About Rigolade

The much awaited fest of Sreenidhi Institute of Science And Technology Rigolade is an annual College fest of Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology (SNIST) conducted by two of the most famous student societies of SNIST, the Arts Club and TechVision. It's a 3 day extravaganza in the campus of SNIST. This fest comprises of creative technical events and

spectacular cultural events.The last day of Rigolade is the cultural night where celebrities attend and grace the event with their prescence and performance. Rigolade fest has a floating population of 3000 students and increases to 11,000 during the night. So, we welcome you to be part of this 3 day college festival in the campus of SNIST to have a best time.




About ARTSCLUB

The club, as an entity, believes in encouraging passion rather than capability. Capability stems up when the heart compels you to generate interest in a particular aspect. With a desire to encourage various forms of art, the club functions with various platforms such as Sargam, Kalakriti, Abhinaya, Nritya,

Shakthi and Sahithi catering to diverse aspects such as painting, film making, writing, acting, singing and dancing.Enhancing in each of the fields, the club has facilitated the students who have had a genuine will to experiment with their penchant.




About THE TechVision CLUB

The club always aims to empower the students with technical skill-set that is highly essential as per the current trends in the industry. We always believe in helping the students to unleash their passion towards coding and assist them with all kind of necessary support. Industry -Institute interaction is

given prime importance. Our events include technical workshops from industry veterans, hackathons and online coding quizzes. We encourage the students, who aim to impact the society in technical aspects to join us and aid them in propelling with their ideas forward
